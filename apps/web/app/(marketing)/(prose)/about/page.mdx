# About Ligala

Ligala is a Philippine legal services platform that connects clients with verified
IBP-member lawyers and gives both sides the tools to run an engagement end-to-end —
intake, fee agreement, case management, invoicing, payments, and referrals — without
spreadsheets and email threads.

## Why we built it

The Philippine legal market is fragmented. Clients struggle to find the right lawyer
for a specific problem; lawyers spend hours on intake, invoicing, and chasing
payments that should take minutes. Ligala is the shared workspace that closes the
loop: clients find verified counsel, lawyers run their practice, and everyone has
the same authoritative record of what was agreed and what was paid.

## What's in the box

- A searchable directory of **verified lawyers** — KYC-checked through IDMeta, with
  IBP chapter, practice areas, jurisdictions, and pro bono availability surfaced.
- **Cases and engagements** — paid and pro bono cases in one place, with digital
  engagements (hourly, flat fee, or contingency) the client signs before work
  begins.
- **Invoicing and payments** — line-item invoices, per-lawyer discount codes,
  PayMongo and PayPal checkout, an immutable transactions ledger, and admin-managed
  refunds.
- **Referrals** — lawyer-to-lawyer case referrals and personal share links that
  attribute new client signups.
- **IBP chapter pages** — a public index of chapters and the verified lawyers in
  each one.

## What we're not doing yet

We deliberately ship narrow. At launch we don't do:

- AI legal research (CD Asia integration is on the roadmap, not the launch).
- Multi-office firms — one office per lawyer, for now.
- Cross-platform mobile apps — the web app is responsive; native apps follow demand.

## Who's behind it
